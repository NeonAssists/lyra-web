# Lyra App Icon Concepts

Three distinct SVG icon concepts for Lyra, a premium music ranking and discovery app. Each explores a different visual approach while maintaining the dark-first, minimal aesthetic inspired by Apple Music and Letterboxd.

---

## Option A: Lyre Abstraction (icon-a.svg)

**Concept:** A stylized lyre rendered in minimal geometric lines—clean, instrumental, and immediately recognizable.

**Design Details:**
- Simplified lyre shape with two curved arms framing seven vertical strings
- Primary accent lines in #6C63FF with varying opacity for depth
- Subtle constellation-like accent dots at the top and sides
- Feels sharp, musical, and timeless

**Why It Works:**
- Direct visual metaphor for "Lyra" (the constellation and the instrument)
- The clean line work feels premium and Apple-esque
- Easily scalable and recognizable at any size
- Strong musical identity without being obvious or clichéd

---

## Option B: Rating + Music Merge (icon-b.svg)

**Concept:** A large typographic rating ("8.4") paired with a small integrated musical note—editorial, modern, and unique.

**Design Details:**
- Premium monospace font (SF Mono style) for the rating number in #6C63FF
- Decimal point as a clean circle element
- Small musical note positioned as a design accent in the upper right
- Horizontal line beneath for editorial polish
- Very magazine-cover energy

**Why It Works:**
- Directly communicates the app's core function: rating music
- Typography-first approach feels sophisticated and editorial (very Letterboxd)
- The integrated note keeps the music element present without overwhelming the design
- Stands out from competitor icons with its unique typographic approach
- Reads well at thumbnail size on app stores

---

## Option C: Lyra Constellation Map (icon-c.svg)

**Concept:** The actual Lyra constellation mapped with stars and connecting lines—cosmic, minimal, and celestial.

**Design Details:**
- Hand-mapped constellation shape (Vega as the brightest central star)
- Thin connecting lines with subtle opacity gradients
- Stars of varying sizes to suggest brightness/hierarchy
- Radial gradient background (dark sky with subtle depth)
- Minimal, premium, and deeply connected to the app's namesake

**Why It Works:**
- Perfect visual anchor to the app's name and mythology
- The constellation gives it a premium, scientific feel
- Dark gradient background adds dimension without distraction
- Works beautifully as both an icon and a system element
- Resonates with people who love music, design, and astronomy

---

## Recommendation

- **Option A** if you want iconic, timeless, and immediately musical
- **Option B** if you want to stand out in the app store with bold typography
- **Option C** if you want premium, connected-to-the-name storytelling

All three maintain dark-first aesthetic, use the #6C63FF accent color consistently, and are sized for 1024x1024 app store icons (iOS will apply corner radius masking automatically).
